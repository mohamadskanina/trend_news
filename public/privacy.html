Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Trend News App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application, which aggregates and filters news from Telegram, Twitter (X), and other sources using their public APIs.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Telegram Integration: We only access Telegram groups or channels that you explicitly join and authorize. We respect your privacy choices and will never access any Telegram content without your explicit permission.

Twitter (X) Integration: We access public posts and trending topics through the Twitter API in accordance with Twitter's Developer Policy. We do not access private accounts or personal messages.

Local Storage: All data fetched from APIs (messages, hashtags, public posts) is stored locally on your device. This data is encrypted and never transmitted to third parties or our servers.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to any third parties. Your information remains private and secure on your device.

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that comply with strict privacy standards, but we do not share personally identifiable information with these services.

4. Cookies and Session Data

Cookies are used only for session continuity and app functionality. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect app functionality.

5. API Compliance

By using Trend News App, you agree to comply with:

  • Telegram API Terms of Service
  • Twitter Developer Policy

We operate in strict compliance with these terms and policies to ensure the security and privacy of all users.

6. Data Security

All data stored on your device is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods. We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your data stored locally in the app
  • Delete your data at any time through the app settings
  • Revoke API authorizations for Telegram and Twitter
  • Opt out of certain data collection features

8. Children's Privacy

Trend News App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
